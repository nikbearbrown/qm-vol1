Measurement & collapse

Prepare a qubit, pick a Pauli observable, and measure. Each shot returns a single eigenvalue ±1 with the Born-rule probability |⟨a|ψ⟩|2, and the state collapses onto that eigenstate. Fire many shots (re-preparing the same state each time) and watch the tallies converge to the theoretical probabilities.
