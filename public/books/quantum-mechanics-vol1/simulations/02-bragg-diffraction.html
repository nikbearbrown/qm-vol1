Electron wavelength from the shortcut λ = 1.226/√V nm. Bragg condition nλ = 2d·sinθn, with θ measured from the planes. Order n exists only when nλ ≤ 2d. At the Davisson–Germer values (54 V, d = 0.091 nm) only the first order survives; raising the voltage shortens λ and brings in the second and third.
