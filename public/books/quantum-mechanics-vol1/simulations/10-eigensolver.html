1D Schrödinger eigensolver

The capstone in miniature. Choose a potential; the solver builds the tridiagonal Hamiltonian (diagonal 2tk+Vj, off-diagonal −tk) on an N = 200 grid and diagonalizes it. Energy levels appear as horizontal lines with their eigenfunctions riding on top, the normalization indicator confirms ∑|ψj|²h = 1, and the table validates the spectrum against the known answer.
